# Traefik Installation and Configuration Guide

---

[Traefik](https://traefik.io/) is a modern, dynamic reverse proxy and load balancer that makes it easy to
manage and route traffic to your applications. It supports various protocols, including HTTP, HTTPS, TCP, and more.
In this guide, I will walk you through the installation and configuration of Traefik on your homelab.
